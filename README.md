# ReVorth

Revorth is a free AI powered thrift scanning tool. Pick up any item — bags, clothing, electronics, furniture, collectibles, anything — point your camera at it and Revorth tells you:

What the item is and its brand
Original retail price when new
Current resale market value
Authentification guide

Built for thrift shoppers, resellers, and anyone who wants to know if they're holding a hidden gem before they buy it.
How It Works

Upload a photo of any thrift item
AI analyzes the item instantly
Get a full valuation in seconds
